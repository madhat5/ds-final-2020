David's Daily Numbers

Data: by date, temperature, weight, hours of sleep

  • Date, range: 4/2020 - 11/2020
  • Temperature, range: 94 - 100 (F)
  • Weight, range: 150 - 170 (lbs)
  • Hours of sleep:
    • < 7hrs: underslept
    • > 7hrs & < 8hrs: regular
